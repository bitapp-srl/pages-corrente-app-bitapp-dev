FAQ

Frequently questions

REGISTRATION

Who can use CORRENTE?

Whoever turned 18 years old.

How do I sign up?

You can register through the application available on App or sito www.corrente.bo.it.

Registration is free and online, without the need to send or recive any paper.

You need a valid driver's licence,insert an identity document,and the details of a credit card, prepaid or otherwise .

At the end of the registration , you will recive a confirmation email at each step.

The validity of your's driver licence and credit card will be active and charged (EUR 0.01 will be charged and than cancelled).

When the profile is fully active, you can start.

With the last email confirming the completion of the registration process, you will recive your personal PIN that you will have to use to start the rentals.

If you have any problems you can contact the Call Center.

For the foreign driving licences, Team CORRENTE reserves up 10 days to validate the registration process.

I am a Company and I would agree with CORRENTE, How Can I do?

Contact our team using the following email: commerciale@corrente.bo.it, we will assist you throughout the process .

RENTAL

How can I rent a car?

Find the nearest vehicle by consulting the map on the APP, or rent the first one you meet on the street. Remember that the reservation is free for the first 15 minutes and it has a maximum duration of 90 minutes.

If you notice that you no longer need the vehicle that you booked or not to make in time to reach the vehicle within 90 minutes , cancel your reservation to make it available to other customers.

How Do I open the rented car?

Once you reach the vehicle simply frame QR Code through the APP or use the “Click & Go” function on the App: The board computer will unlock the doors the ignition card will already be inside the car, inside the object compartment.

If you find the vehicle damaged, dirty or if the keys are missing , report with the App or call the Customer Service.

If you feel that the vehicle is not suitable for use, you can decide to release it and choose another one.

How do I drive the car?

Car are very useful to drive: The ignition card is in the object compartment and must be inserted in the slot in front of the gearshift lever.

You must enter the PIN code on the App.

Cars have the automatic transmission (left foot not needed!). To start them you must leave the lever in the position P, hold down the brake pedal and press the START button located on the dashboard, to the right of the steering wheel. Once you start the cars will indicate the status with the word READY on the dashboard ( they are very quiet and you may not notice the ignition of the engine). R is used for backing down, N is the equivalent of putting the car in neutral, D is the forward motion.

Where can I drive?

With Corrente you can drive in ZTL and on the priority lanes, respecting the Highway Code.You can't circulate in pedestrian areas and in the areas dedicated exclusively to taxis and public transport (Archiginnasio, Azzo Gardino, Piazza Medaglie d'Oro e Piazza Aldrovandi, you can check it on the map !). Also remember that in Bologna on Saturdays, Sundays and public holidays there are T’DAYS and the pedestrian Area expands. If you have other doubts you can visit the section: How does it work , or contact us.

How many miles can I drive?

Cars charged at 100% have an autonomy of about 300 chilometres,which is closely related to the driving style of the car.

When you hire a car Corrente, you will always have an indication of the autonomy in km of the car on the app and inside the car on the dashboard.

Remember not to leave your car with less than 30 km of the autonomy!

Where can I park?

To end the rental, park the vehicle whitin the coverage area and press the button "End the rental".

You can park on the blue stripes for free and also in places reserved for residents.

Remember not to leave the vehicle in an area where it is not possible to detect the GPS signal and/or the GSM signal, tipically in the underground garadge, if not in places indicated by the Operator.

The vehicle,moreover, cannot be released on parking area with daily or hourly limitation.

You can aldo park your car outside the service coverage areas but only in "Stop" modality, during which the car is still in your availability and the running count continues.

Before you get out the car, check that you have closed the windows, turned off the lights and pulled the handbrake and put the ignition card in the object compartment.

How do I know if I am outside the release area?

You can always check your location and view the operating area using the app Corrente.

Do I need to recharge the cars?

No, we take care of recharged the cars. When cars are without charge, are not available for the rental.

How do I pay for the Car-sharing service?

The amount will be immediately charged on your card at the end of the rental.

At the end of the rental, you will recive a summary email that contains the following data: n° rental, license plate, place and time of start and end of rental, lenght of release ( the minutes are considered partially used for rounding of sixty seconds starting from the 31st second), total mileage and total amount charged.

Are there any additional costs?

No, the rental has a Tariff per minute and it does not provide any extra cost.

What if I take a fine or make an accident ?

In the event of the driver has encountered any fines, these are at his own expense.

The vehicles are insured but in case of an accident , if the liability was imputed to the driver who rented the car, he will be imposed a charge of maximum amount equal to the deductible of 500 €.

What happens if there is a problem with the payment?

If the amount due cannot be charged, your account will be temporarily blocked and other attempts will be made to charge the amount from your payment profile.
